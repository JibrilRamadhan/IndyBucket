# Indy Bucket - Brand Identity Documentation

## 🎨 Brand Overview

**Brand Name:** Indy Bucket  
**Tagline:** "Redefine the Art of Gifting"  
**Industry:** Premier Gift Boutique (Specialized Bucket Presentations)  
**Core Offering:** Unique gift buckets combining flowers, money presentations, snacks, and premium surprises

---

## 🎯 Brand Positioning

Indy Bucket is NOT a traditional florist. We are a **specialized gift boutique** that creates unforgettable moments through curated bucket presentations. Each bucket is a masterpiece combining:

- 💐 Premium flowers
- 💵 Creative money presentations
- 🍫 Gourmet snacks & treats
- 🎁 Thoughtful surprises

---

## 🎨 Visual Identity

### Logo
**Design:** Reddish-brown floral line art icon (8-petal flower design)
- **Format:** SVG inline for scalability
- **Color:** #7A503D (earthy reddish-brown)
- **Placement:** 
  - Header: Above brand name
  - Footer: Left side with brand name

### Color Palette

#### Primary Colors
- **Background:** `#FDF9F4` (Soft cream)
- **Primary Accent:** `#7A503D` (Earthy reddish-brown)
- **Primary Container:** `#7A503D` (Buttons, footer background)
- **Text Primary:** `#1c1c19` (Dark brown-black)
- **Text Muted:** `#8C7164` (Muted brown-gray)

#### Semantic Colors
- **Surface:** `#fdf9f4`
- **Surface Container:** `#f1ede8`
- **Outline:** `#83746e`
- **Secondary:** `#74584a`

### Typography

#### Font Families
- **Serif (Headings):** Playfair Display (600, 700)
  - Brand name
  - Major headlines (Hero, Section titles)
  
- **Sans-serif (Body):** DM Sans (400, 600)
  - Body text
  - Prices
  - Labels
  - Navigation

#### Font Scale
- `display-lg`: 64px / 1.1 line-height (Hero headlines)
- `headline-lg`: 48px / 1.2 line-height (Section titles)
- `headline-md`: 32px / 1.3 line-height (Brand name)
- `headline-sm`: 24px / 1.4 line-height (Product names)
- `body-lg`: 18px / 1.6 line-height (Hero description)
- `body-md`: 16px / 1.6 line-height (Body text)
- `label-lg`: 14px / 1.0 line-height (Buttons, nav)
- `label-md`: 12px / 1.0 line-height (Small labels)

## 📝 Brand Voice & Messaging

### Key Messages
1. **"Redefine the Art of Gifting"** (Hero headline)
2. **"Create Unforgettable Moments"** (CTA)
3. **"Premier Gift Boutique"** (Not "florist")
4. **"Signature Bucket Presentations"** (Core offering)

### Tone of Voice
- **Premium** but approachable
- **Specialized** and expert
- **Creative** and thoughtful
- **Warm** and personal

### Vocabulary Guidelines
✅ **Use:**
- Bucket / Bucket presentation
- Premier gift boutique
- Curated / Signature
- Unforgettable moments
- Specialized

❌ **Avoid:**
- Florist / Flower shop
- Generic / Standard
- Simple / Basic
- Ordinary

## 🎨 Design Principles

1. **Clean & Premium:** Ample whitespace, sophisticated layout
2. **Warm & Earthy:** Reddish-brown accent color throughout
3. **Specialized Focus:** Every element reinforces "bucket" concept
4. **Immersive Experience:** Large images, arch frames, smooth transitions
5. **Consistent Branding:** Logo and color palette in every section
